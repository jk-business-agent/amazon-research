Degraded-mode fallback — Amazon direct pages blocked all session: Every direct Amazon.com page fetch (amazon.com/gp/movers-and-shakers, amazon.com/Best-Sellers/zgbs, and all category variants) returned HTTP 403 Forbidden on every attempt. No Amazon page content was retrieved via WebFetch. All product discovery, verification, and ranking data was derived from WebSearch result snippets and secondary press, trade, and retail-analytics coverage. This is logged per workflow protocol and may mean some products climbing rapidly on Amazon's own internal mover charts were missed entirely.

Verification rule: VERIFIED = corroborated by at least 2 independent sources (Amazon listing URL + at least one independent press article, trade publication, or retail analytics report). LOW-CONFIDENCE = single source found or second source not accessible during this session. No confidence label was silently upgraded. All specific numbers (percentages, prices, review counts, revenue figures) were drawn from source snippets and not inferred or invented.
