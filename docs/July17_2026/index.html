Methodology & Sources

Research conducted July 17, 2026 by AI-assisted pipeline processing the sibling agent's July 16, 2026 Amazon report. 148 sources checked across 17 products and 31 alternatives. 4 Electronics products excluded per standing research policy.

VERIFIED status requires at least two independent corroborating sources for US manufacturing or assembly — a company's own website claim alone never qualifies. Corroborating sources used: independent certification bodies (CCOF, NASC, FSC, NFS International, AbilityOne/NAEPB), press coverage naming specific domestic facilities, state economic development records, federal procurement databases, and factory tour documentation. LOW-CONFIDENCE is assigned when only a single source is available or the manufacturing claim could not be cross-verified.

Display order: NEW before RECURRING; confirmed $25–$100 price band before outside-band or unconfirmed; higher estimated repurchase frequency before lower. Rolling 14-day product history managed via resources/seen-products-history.json. 7 entries pruned this run (last seen before July 3, 2026).

Structural gaps are declared only after exhaustive search across a minimum of 5 candidate brands within the category finds no credible domestic alternative.
