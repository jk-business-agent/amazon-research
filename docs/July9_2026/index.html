Executive Summary

With Prime Day 2026 now two weeks past (it ran June 23–26), Amazon's charts for July 9 reflect the classic mid-summer double pull: back-to-school supplies and summer-heat cooling products are both climbing fast, producing 9 new entrants across 8 categories — the most new entrants since the June 25 Prime Day report. The Dreame L60 Ultra PE Robot Vacuum stands out as the single most notable new entrant, earning the #1 overall position on Vacuum Wars' July 2026 rankings after in-depth testing, signaling that hot-water-mop self-cleaning (212°F / 100°C) and 30,000Pa suction power have crossed the mainstream affordability threshold. Simultaneously, DREO — Amazon's stated #1 household fan brand for three consecutive years — is expanding into true refrigerant-based portable ACs, a meaningful step beyond the evaporative coolers that dominated last week's report. Amazon's newest Fire TV Stick 4K Plus (AI-powered search, Wi-Fi 6), with over 109,000 reviews at a 4.6-star average, holds its top streaming-device position with no active sale event driving it — pure organic demand. Back-to-school supplies account for 5 of today's 17 products (pastel highlighters, Mead composition books, the JanSport Classic Backpack, Crayola art sets, and the Kindle Paperwhite), confirming that early July marks the visible inflection point for BTS buying.
