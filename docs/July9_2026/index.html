Executive Summary

Methodology & Sources

Source: Sibling agent's Amazon trending products report for July 9, 2026. 17 products parsed; 5 Electronics excluded per standing policy. 12 non-Electronics products researched.

VERIFIED requires two or more independent sources confirming American ownership AND (where claimed) domestic manufacturing — e.g., state business filings + press coverage + facility records. The company's own website or marketing copy alone is never sufficient for VERIFIED status.

LOW-CONFIDENCE means the alternative is plausibly American-owned or American-made but could not be confirmed by two independent sources within the search scope, or the company's own claims were the only documentation available.

Disqualifications this cycle: Paula's Choice (acquired by Unilever, UK, August 2021); Friedrich Air Conditioning (acquired by Rheem/Paloma Industries, Japan, 2021); Frigidaire (Swedish Electrolux parent); iRobot (acquired by Shenzhen PICEA Robotics, China, January 2026); Neato Robotics (shut down 2023); SharkNinja (Cayman Islands incorporated, ~38.6% Chinese shareholder control per SEC 20-F filings).

Display order: NEW products before RECURRING; within each group, products in the $25–$100 band and higher repurchase frequency appear first.

Format gap disclosure (Pastel Pens): No American manufacturer produces a 39-piece pastel gel pen/highlighter bundle. Alternatives represent the closest American-made writing instrument equivalents with an explicit format gap noted.

Rolling history: Products are tracked across a 14-day window. Items last seen more than 14 days ago are pruned from the history file. 13 entries pruned this cycle (last seen before June 25, 2026).

Sources checked (~58 total): Company websites, state business registries (WI, TX, NY, MN, CA, CO, IL, PA, NJ, MA), USDA FSIS facility records, SEC/EDGAR filings, AAFCO compliance records, affiliate network listings (ShareASale, CJ, Impact, Rakuten), and independent press (Wirecutter, Outside Magazine, Forbes, TechCrunch, pet industry trade press).
