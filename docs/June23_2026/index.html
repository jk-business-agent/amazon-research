Executive Summary

This run is based on the sibling Competitor Research Agent's June 22, 2026 report, which listed 18 Amazon top-sellers across 11 categories. 2 were hard-excluded as Electronics (Amazon Fire TV Stick, Apple Watch Series 11), leaving 16 products across 10 categories carried forward. Comparing against the rolling 14-day product-history log, 11 are NEW since June 20 and 5 are RECURRING (Pampers Swaddlers Diapers Size 5, Owala FreeSip Water Bottle, Medicube Zero Pore Pad 2.0, CELSIUS Variety Pack, and Dr. Elsey's Clumping Cat Litter all reappeared in the sibling report). Six parallel research agents found 68 American-made alternatives total — 28 cleared the two-source VERIFIED bar and 40 remain LOW-CONFIDENCE (a single, uncorroborated company claim). Two internal inconsistencies surfaced during this run's quality pass and were corrected before publishing: a Medicube alternative ("Studio Gear") was tagged VERIFIED by its research agent despite that agent's own prose flagging it LOW-CONFIDENCE — the tag was corrected, leaving Medicube at 0 VERIFIED / 4 LOW-CONFIDENCE; and Purina Pro Plan's research summary undercounted its own VERIFIED tally (it actually found 5 VERIFIED domestic pet-food makers, not 4) — corrected to 5 VERIFIED / 1 LOW-CONFIDENCE, the single strongest VERIFIED showing of any product this run. The pet-food and pet-litter categories combined show the deepest credible domestic supply found to date (7 VERIFIED alternatives across Purina and Dr. Elsey's). By contrast, security cameras and AI baby monitors remain the thinnest: only 1 of 3 Ring Spotlight Cam alternatives and 0 of 2 Smart AI Baby Monitor alternatives cleared VERIFIED, with hardware manufacturing location undisclosed industry-wide for the latter. The Owala water-bottle category again shows no maker producing a true vacuum-insulated stainless-steel bottle that is both fully VERIFIED and a precise category match — all three alternatives carry either a partial-claim or category-departure caveat, consistent with the prior report's finding. ANRABESS itself is confirmed Chinese-manufactured (it is the Amazon product being compared against, not an alternative); its strongest American-made apparel alternative, Los Angeles Apparel, carries a documented 2020 labor-safety controversy worth disclosing alongside its otherwise clean domestic-manufacturing record.
