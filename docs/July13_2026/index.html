Methodology & Sources

Research approach: All findings were gathered using WebSearch and WebFetch only (no paid API), per workflow specification. Amazon.com direct pages — including Best Sellers (zgbs), Movers & Shakers, and New Releases — returned HTTP 403 Forbidden on every direct fetch attempt this session. This is consistent with all prior daily runs in this archive. Research fell back to secondary press coverage, retail analytics aggregators, and consumer editorial sources per the workflow's degraded-mode protocol.

Verification standard: VERIFIED = corroborated by at least 2 independent sources (editorial, aggregator, or review publication — not two Amazon URL variants). LOW-CONFIDENCE = only Amazon-derived listing sources confirmed (Bunch O Balloons), with no independent editorial source confirming current rank. For RECURRING items whose verification was established in prior daily runs (Apple Watch Series 11, Dreame L60 Ultra PE, etc.), prior-run sourcing is maintained and noted.
