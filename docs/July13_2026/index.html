Executive Summary

17 non-Electronics products from the sibling agent's July 13, 2026 Amazon report (3 Electronics excluded) yielded 34 American business alternatives across 9 categories: 26 VERIFIED and 8 LOW-CONFIDENCE. 9 products are NEW this cycle; 8 are RECURRING. The standout NEW finds are Hello Bello (Waco, TX — verified American-made diapers, actively upgraded 312,000 sq ft factory now at >95% on-time fulfillment) as the best head-to-head Pampers alternative, and Sunwarrior (St. George, UT — CCOF-certified organic facility, fully American-manufactured vegan protein) as the lead alternative to Nestlé-owned Orgain. The DREO Tower Fan category surfaces Lasko (est. 1906, Franklin TN + Fort Worth TX assembly) as a genuinely American-assembled tower fan alternative. Two categories remain confirmed market gaps: premium electric water guns (SpyraFour) and rapid-fill self-sealing water balloons (Bunch O Balloons) have no American-manufactured alternatives. The Beauty & Personal Care category is the most represented with 3 NEW products appearing simultaneously (LANEIGE, Medicube Zero Pore Pad, Medicube Age-R), reflecting the Korean skincare surge in U.S. Amazon rankings.
